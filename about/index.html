Will is a senior at Yale University interested in the intersection of language and computation. This includes the algorithmic foundations of linguistic structure, dynamics of linguistic change, and computational challenges in modern NLP. He has worked on research investigating:

  • How can linguistics and formal language theory inform the state of the art in NLP, and vice versa?
  • How can machine learning be applied to language decipherment and other historical linguistic problems?
  • Deep learning and video games.

Outside of class, Will is the captain of the Yale Dota 2 team and a member of Yale Table Tennis. He is also creating an indie video game called the Book of Thoth and enjoys rap.
